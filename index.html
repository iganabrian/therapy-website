About Us

We are trusted by millions of people

Welcome to a safe, compassionate space where healing begins.Our experienced therapists are here to walk alongside you through life’s challenges — whether you’re navigating anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, trauma, or simply seeking greater self-understanding. We believe therapy is not about “fixing” you; it’s about helping you reconnect with your own strength, clarity, and resilience. Using evidence-based approaches tailored to your unique needs, we create a warm, non-judgmental environment where you can speak openly and grow at your own pace. You don’t have to face it alone. Take the first step toward feeling lighter, more grounded, and more hopeful. Contact us today to schedule your initial consultation. Your well-being matters.

Our team

Dr. Zawadi M.

Medical Psychologist (PhD, PsyD)

Dr. Zawadi M., Medical Psychologist, specializes in the mind-body connection during illness. She helps patients manage chronic pain, cancer, heart disease, neurological disorders, and surgical recovery through evidence-based therapies like CBT, ACT, mindfulness, and motivational interviewing. Warm, culturally attuned care. Telehealth and in-person. New patients welcome.

Dr. Summer S.

Board-Certified Internal Medicine Physician

Dr. Summer S. provides compassionate, comprehensive primary care with a focus on prevention, women’s health, chronic disease management, and mental well-being. Skilled in diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders, and anxiety/depression screening. Same-day appointments, telehealth, and a warm, patient-centered approach. Your health partner for life. New patients welcome.
